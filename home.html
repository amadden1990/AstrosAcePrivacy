Welcome to Astro's Ace

Astro's Ace is an exhilarating mixed-reality game designed exclusively for the Meta Quest 3, inspired by the classic arcade game Asteroids. In this futuristic reimagining, players don the role of Astro, a daring space pilot tasked with navigating through treacherous asteroid fields. Using the immersive capabilities of the Meta Quest 3, players will experience the thrill of space combat like never before, seamlessly blending their real-world surroundings with a dynamic cosmic battlefield.

Equipped with state-of-the-art weaponry and agile spacecraft, players must dodge and destroy asteroids, face off against enemy ships, and collect power-ups to enhance their abilities. The game features stunning visuals, intuitive controls, and fun engaging gameplay that will keep players on the edge of their seats. Whether you're a veteran of the original Asteroids or new to the genre, Astro's Ace promises an out-of-this-world adventure that pushes the boundaries of mixed-reality gaming.
